About Me

Hello! I'm Tyrni, a somewhat experienced entrepreneur and a highly skilled team player in veterinary care. With years of experience in changing tasks, problem-solving, and performing precise work, I am adept at handling any challenge that comes my way.

In 2023, I completed AW Academy's training program, which focused on Full-Stack Development and Azure. AW Academy uses an accelerated learning method that provides a fast path to a new profession for individuals who demonstrate above-average learning ability, strong motivation, and suitable skills and traits for consultant work. The program's multi-stage selection process ensures that only the most qualified individuals are accepted.
